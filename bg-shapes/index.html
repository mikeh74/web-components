Manchester is a city steeped in sporting tradition, and we aren’t just talking red versus blue. We’ve got the National Cycling Centre, Manchester Aquatics Centre and so much more. Basically, there’s no better place to do a sports and exercise science degree and gain all the skills you’ll need for an exciting career in this sector.

If you’re passionate about sport and physical activity, we have a wide range of sports courses that’ll help you build the knowledge, practical skills and understanding of sports you’ll need to excel in a career in sports. Covering everything from sports science and nutrition to fitness, sports biomechanics and sports coaching, we’ll make sure you’re in the best possible shape to compete against the elite and stand out from the crowd when you start your career.

What’s more, you’ll be among the very first students to learn in our brand-new, state-of-the-art Manchester sports facilities – including a match analysis suite, indoor sports hall and 3G pitch – at our redeveloped Openshaw Campus.

We’re committed to providing all our students with a career-focused learning experience and as a student of our School of Sport, Health and Wellbeing you’ll learn in industry-standard, state-of-the-art facilities at our Openshaw Campus, which will provide the perfect environment for you to excel.
